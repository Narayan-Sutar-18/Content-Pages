What is CNG?
Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) is an eco-friendly automotive fuel used in vehicles. It produces lower emissions compared to petrol and diesel.
Is CNG safe for vehicles?
Yes. CNG cylinders are designed and tested to withstand high pressure and are safer compared to conventional fuels when used correctly.
How can I convert my vehicle to CNG?
Vehicle conversion must be done only at authorized CNG retrofitment centers approved by the respective RTO.
What documents are required for CNG conversion?
Vehicle RC, valid insurance, PUC certificate, and owner identification documents are typically required.
How often should CNG cylinder be tested?
CNG cylinders should be hydro-tested periodically as per statutory norms to ensure continued safety.
