What is PNG?
Piped Natural Gas (PNG) is a clean and convenient fuel supplied through underground pipelines directly to households and establishments.
How can I apply for a new PNG connection?
Customers can apply online by submitting the application form along with the required documents through the official portal.
Is PNG safe for domestic use?
Yes. PNG is lighter than air and disperses quickly in case of leakage, making it safer when proper safety measures are followed.
What documents are required for PNG connection?
Proof of identity, address proof, ownership or tenancy documents, and a recent photograph are typically required.
How is PNG billing done?
PNG billing is based on actual gas consumption measured in Standard Cubic Meters (SCM) as recorded by the gas meter.
